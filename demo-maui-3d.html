Honua Maui in 3D — buildings & terrain

Overture Maps building footprints extruded by their real height attributes over USGS 3DEP terrain — vector tiles and a terrarium DEM served by Honua Server at demo.honua.io, rendered in 2.5D through the Apache 2.0 Honua JS SDK scene runtime. Drag with right-click (or two fingers) to tilt; click a building for its attributes.
