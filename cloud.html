// what honua cloud is

Everything Honua is. Nothing to operate.

You get the full Honua feature set — every protocol, every edition — without standing up or maintaining any infrastructure. We handle provisioning, upgrades, backups, and observability. You get an endpoint.

Same server, same protocols
GeoServices REST, OGC APIs, WMS/WFS/WCS/WMTS, OData, gRPC, MCP — the full protocol surface, hosted. Your existing clients point at a new URL and keep working.
Cost scales with workload, not adoption
No per-user fees. No named-user seats. The same capacity-band pricing model as self-hosted — your invoice reflects your workload, not your headcount.
Operated for you
Provisioning, upgrades, backups, health monitoring, and failover — handled. Your team focuses on data and applications, not infrastructure.
Bring your data, keep your exits
PostGIS stays yours. The open protocols are your exit — switching to self-hosted or a different provider is a URL change, not a migration project.
