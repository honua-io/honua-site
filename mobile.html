Mobile

Field collection without per-user licensing.

Honua includes a .NET MAUI mobile toolkit for offline maps, forms, sync, background upload, and gRPC transport — with AR and IoT-ready field workflows.

Capabilities

What your field teams get.

Offline

GeoPackage-backed storage

Offline maps, queued edits, sync cursors, and field data live in a standards-friendly GeoPackage workflow that works without connectivity.

Transport

gRPC-first access

Binary transport for large feature retrieval and sync flows. Dramatically less bandwidth and faster parsing than XML/REST patterns.

Framework

MAUI cross-platform

Built on .NET MAUI with iOS, Android, and Windows support for teams already in the Microsoft ecosystem.

Field UX

Forms and record workflows

Dynamic forms, validation, calculated fields, duplicate detection, and record lifecycle management for real field operations.

AR + IoT

Beyond basic forms

AR-assisted field collection and sensor-aware workflows with Bluetooth LE, LoRa, and WiFi device integration. First open-source offering of its kind.

Sync

Background upload and conflict handling

Connectivity-aware background sync with configurable conflict resolution strategies. Works in the field, not just on conference WiFi.

Performance

gRPC changes what mobile GIS feels like.

Roughly 60–70% less bandwidth and up to 5× faster parsing than REST/XML mobile patterns. Strong types mean fewer serialization errors. The difference shows up in battery life, sync speed, and field reliability.

Open Source

The mobile toolkit is Apache 2.0.

Evaluate, fork, and build on the mobile SDK without client-side licensing ambiguity. Same open license as the JavaScript, .NET, and Python SDKs.
