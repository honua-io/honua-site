Mobile

Field workflows are part of the platform.

Honua includes a MAUI-first mobile lane with offline GeoPackage sync, forms, background upload, and gRPC-first performance for operational teams.

Mobile Positioning

What the mobile surface includes.

Offline

GeoPackage-backed storage

Offline maps, queued edits, sync cursors, and field data all live in a standards-friendly GeoPackage workflow.

Transport

gRPC-first when it matters

Unary and streaming access patterns support large feature retrieval and collaboration flows with lower overhead than pure REST polling.

Framework

MAUI-first cross-platform

The current mobile lane is rooted in .NET MAUI with iOS, Android, and Windows support, which makes it legible to enterprise app teams.

Field UX

Forms and record workflows

Dynamic form schema, validation, calculated fields, duplicate detection, and record lifecycle support define the field workflow story.

Sync

Background upload and conflict handling

Connectivity-aware background sync and conflict strategies turn mobile from a demo into an operational lane.

Economics

No per-user mobile tax

Mobile workflows do not carry a separate per-seat field subscription tax.
