Field Toolkit

Bring offline maps, sync, and field workflows onto the same platform.

Honua includes a MAUI-based field toolkit for teams that need offline work, forms, sync, and device-friendly map interaction without layering on another seat-priced field product.

What It Covers

Use the same platform for field collection and central services.

Offline Work

Store maps and edits locally when the network is unreliable.

Use GeoPackage-backed storage, queued edits, and sync cursors so field teams can keep working when connectivity drops.

Sync

Move updates back to the platform without brittle manual processes.

Background upload, conflict handling, and gRPC-friendly transport help the field workflow feel operational instead of fragile.

Forms

Guide collection with validation, calculated fields, and workflow rules.

Build forms and record flows that match real field work instead of stopping at a generic data-entry screen.

Why Teams Like It

The field toolkit is part of the platform, not a separate licensing silo.

Operational Fit

Field work stays close to the same services and data model the platform already uses.

Teams do not need to shuttle data through an extra vendor surface just to support offline collection and sync.

Commercial Fit

Growing the field team does not force a new seat conversation every time.

The field toolkit fits the same open-core platform story instead of introducing a new per-user licensing line.

Toolkit Components

What ships in the field toolkit.

Component Purpose Use
Transport and auth Connect field apps to Honua services Supports service access, sync, and secure runtime integration.
Offline engine Store maps and edits locally Keeps data usable when field conditions are unstable.
Forms and workflows Guide collection and record handling Supports validation, calculated fields, and field process rules.
MAUI integration Build across iOS, Android, and Windows Lets teams work from one codebase for common field scenarios.
