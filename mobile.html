Field Toolkit

Beta field workflows on the same runtime boundary.

The MAUI-first field toolkit is Beta and pilot-scoped for Preview messaging. GeoPackage-backed storage, sync, and form workflows should not be presented as Preview-ready until field proof assets are linked.

Beta scope

Offline storage, sync, and guided collection targets.

Offline

GeoPackage storage with queued edits.

Maps, features, and edits are target capabilities for pilot validation. Field teams should confirm coverage-gap behavior against their own proof run.

Sync

Background upload with conflict handling.

gRPC-friendly transport, sync cursors, and conflict handling remain Beta scope until repeatable mobile proof assets are attached.

Forms

Validation, calculated fields, workflow rules.

Collection forms with required photos, conditional sections, and derived values are pilot-scoped field workflow targets.

Same platform, all the way to the device

Field tools that share the runtime, not copy it.

Shared data model

The schema your analysts use is the schema your field app uses.

Collections, field definitions, and validation rules are intended to flow from Honua into MAUI clients, with pilot validation required for each field workflow.

Shared operations

The same auth, traces, and rollout pipeline.

Mobile requests are intended to flow through the same gRPC runtime and surface in the same OpenTelemetry traces. Keep this as Beta scope until deployment evidence is linked.

Beta / roadmap scope

Toolkit components under pilot validation.
