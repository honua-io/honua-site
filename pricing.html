Pricing and Licensing

Pricing that scales with deployment depth, not user count.

Honua uses an open-core model with clear runtime boundaries: community is free, commercial is quote-based, and the package structure is public. Teams do not pay more because they added more viewers, more field staff, more standards, more SDKs, or more agent workflows.

Commercial pricing is structured around three buckets: software subscription, support tier, and optional professional services.

Commercial Model

Simple enough for buyers to understand quickly.

Community

Free self-hosted use under ELv2 for evaluation, internal deployment, and smaller production footprints.

Pro

Quote-based per-node commercial subscription when a team needs coordinated scale, streaming depth, and stronger runtime capability.

Enterprise

Annual contract for governance, premium support, change management, compliance controls, and multi-team production requirements.

What stays public

No per-user fees, plus protocols, SDKs, mobile tooling, and the base MCP data-access layer remain visible and adoptable across the platform.

How Buying Works

The boundary is explicit.

Category How Honua treats it
Users and field staff Not monetized per seat. Adding analysts, viewers, operators, and field workers should not trigger a relicensing event.
Protocols and SDKs Not hidden behind add-ons. GeoServices REST, OGC APIs, OData, SDKs, mobile tooling, and base MCP are part of adoption.
Commercial upsell Focused on coordinated scale, governance, support, streaming depth, and enterprise controls rather than artificial product crippling.
Deployment costs Customer cloud spend, managed database spend, and professional services stay separate unless explicitly bundled in an order form.

Private Operator Layer

The AI operator tool is separate by design.

Public

Runtime, protocols, SDKs, mobile, and base MCP

The open-core promise covers the server runtime, protocol APIs, official SDKs, mobile tooling, and the base MCP data-access layer.

Private

AI DevOps/operator copilot

Operator-grade rollout planning, delegated operations, and implementation-partner workflows can remain private enterprise tooling on top of the public admin APIs.

Budgeting

Commercial pricing is quote-based today, not hidden.

Honua is explicit that community is free, commercial packaging is public, and final commercial pricing is still quote-based while the launch motion matures.
