// privacy & cookies

Limited site data, explicit consent, and clear contact handling.

Last updated: June 10, 2026

honua.io is a product and documentation site. We only collect the information needed to respond to inbound contact requests and, if you accept analytics, understand the site path that led to them.

What we collect

  • Contact form submissions. Name, work email, company, message, and consented lead-attribution context submitted through the site contact form.
  • Optional analytics. Page-view and site-interaction analytics load only after you accept analytics cookies.
  • Session attribution. After analytics consent, landing page, referrer, UTM campaign fields, and the most recent CTA may be stored for the current browser session.
  • Basic server logs. Standard hosting and security logs may capture IP address, browser details, and request metadata.
  • No account requirement. You can read the site and public docs without creating an account or providing personal data.

How we use it

DataPurpose
Contact form dataRespond to questions, schedule follow-up, and handle early-access or commercial discussions.
Lead-attribution contextAfter analytics consent, understand which site path, campaign, or CTA led to an inquiry so follow-up can stay relevant.
Consented analyticsUnderstand which pages help or confuse buyers so the site can improve over time.
Security & hosting logsDetect abuse, troubleshoot delivery issues, and keep the site available.

Cookies · analytics only loads after consent

The site shows a cookie banner before analytics loads. Accepting analytics enables Google Analytics for this site. Declining keeps the site functional and skips analytics cookies. CTA and contact-form attribution use current-session browser storage only after that consent.

Use the button below to clear the current cookie preference and show the banner again.

Retention & sharing

  • Retention. Contact inquiries are retained for follow-up, commercial discussion, and basic relationship history.
  • Processors. The site currently relies on FormSubmit for contact-form handling and Google Analytics only after consent.
  • No sale of personal data. Honua does not sell contact data collected through the site.
  • Questions. For site privacy questions, email info@honua.io.
