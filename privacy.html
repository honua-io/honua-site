Privacy and Cookies

Limited site data, explicit consent, and clear contact handling.

honua.io is a product and documentation site. We only collect the information needed to respond to inbound contact requests and, if you consent, basic site analytics.

What We Collect

Only the data needed to run the site and answer inquiries.

Contact form submissions

Name, work email, company, and the message you submit through the site contact form.

Optional analytics

Page-view and site-interaction analytics load only after you accept analytics cookies.

Basic server logs

Standard hosting and security logs may capture IP address, browser details, and request metadata.

No account requirement

You can read the site and public docs without creating an account or providing personal data.

How We Use It

Inquiry handling, site improvement, and security.

Data Purpose
Contact form data Respond to questions, schedule follow-up, and handle early-access or commercial discussions.
Consented analytics Understand which pages help or confuse buyers so the site can improve over time.
Security and hosting logs Detect abuse, troubleshoot delivery issues, and keep the site available.

Cookies

Analytics only loads after consent.

The site shows a cookie banner before analytics loads. Accepting analytics enables Google Analytics for this site. Declining keeps the site functional and skips analytics cookies.

Essential behavior

No analytics before consent.

Honua does not load Google Analytics until you click Accept analytics in the cookie banner.

Cookie control

Reset the choice any time.

Use the button below to clear the current cookie preference and show the banner again.

Retention and Sharing

We keep inquiry data only as long as it remains useful.

Retention

Contact inquiries are retained for follow-up, commercial discussion, and basic relationship history.

Processors

The site currently relies on FormSubmit for contact-form handling and Google Analytics only after consent.

No sale of personal data

Honua does not sell contact data collected through the site.

Questions

For site privacy questions, email mike@honua.io.
