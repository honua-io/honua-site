What this shows

  • community OData v4 feed with $apply aggregation — groupby, aggregate, filter, compute, and geo.intersects() extent filters
  • community Vector tiles (MVT) drawing the parcels the charts are filtering — chart→map sync is a client-side tile filter, no round trip
  • community GeoParquet / GeoArrow / GeoJSON export from the same query surface (f=parquet)
  • pro Spatial analytics endpoints — clustering, spatial join, buffer aggregate, density binning. Not used on this page: everything here runs on Community-tier OData
