Workflow support

AI enablement across GIS work, not a chatbot beside it.

Workflow How Honua supports it Why it matters
Spatial query Agents can inspect schemas, run filters, buffers, intersections, joins, and exports through structured tools. Non-specialists can ask spatial questions while governed data and permissions stay intact.
AI DevOps Operational agents can read deploy status, logs, traces, config, and runbook actions. Operators get help diagnosing routine incidents without bypassing review.
Analytics and reports Agents can combine spatial queries, summaries, tables, maps, citations, and exports into repeatable reports. GIS teams spend less time assembling routine outputs and more time reviewing analysis quality.
App creation Typed SDKs, live schemas, and service metadata give agents the inputs needed to generate spatial apps. Developers can turn governed spatial services into usable applications faster.
Cartography and map making Agents can use layers, styles, legends, filters, and layout context as explicit platform objects. Map production becomes more accessible while GIS leads retain standards and review.
